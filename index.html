ℹ  Methodology & data sources
Projections are estimates based on publicly available industry data and the inputs provided. Fee revenue figures represent gross customer-facing revenue and do not deduct network operator fees (industry average approximately $0.04–$0.50 per transaction). All projected figures represent a ±10% estimated range around the model midpoint. Where a receive-side fee is charged, the model assumes full market penetration regardless of fee level; actual adoption may vary.
